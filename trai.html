Televisions

A television ad, or TV commercial, is a short promotional video aired during breaks in TV programming to market a product, service, or idea. These ads typically last between 15 to 60 seconds and use a mix of visuals, sound, and storytelling to grab attention and leave a lasting impression. They're designed to reach a wide audience quickly and are often crafted to be emotionally engaging, memorable, and persuasive
